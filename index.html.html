Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care from routine cleanings to advanced restorative and cosmetic dentistry.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional scaling and polishing removes plaque, tartar, and surface stains. Recommended every 6 months to prevent gum disease and cavities. Includes a full oral health assessment.

Book Now →

Teeth Whitening

Our advanced in-office whitening system delivers results up to 8 shades brighter in a single 60-minute session. Safe, effective, and long-lasting with professional-grade bleaching agents.

Book Now →

Dental Implants

Titanium implants that integrate with your jawbone for a permanent, natural-looking replacement. Restores full chewing function and prevents bone loss. Single, multiple, and full-arch solutions available.

Book Now →

Braces & Orthodontics

Traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners (Invisalign-style) to correct crowding, gaps, and bite problems. We provide orthodontic care for teens and adults alike.

Book Now →

Root Canal Treatment

Modern endodontic therapy that removes infected pulp tissue and seals the tooth to prevent further infection. Done under local anesthesia for a comfortable, pain-free experience.

Book Now →

General Dentistry

Comprehensive checkups, digital X-rays, tooth-colored fillings, extractions, and preventive treatments. The foundation of good oral health for every member of your family.

Book Now →

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care designed to build positive experiences from a young age. Preventive treatments, sealants, and fluoride applications to protect growing smiles.

Book Now →

Crowns & Bridges

Porcelain and zirconia crowns that restore damaged teeth to their original shape and strength. Bridges close gaps with natural-looking artificial teeth anchored to adjacent teeth.

Book Now →

Dentures

Full and partial removable dentures customized for a comfortable fit and natural appearance. Modern materials ensure durability and a confident, functional smile.

Book Now →

Our Story

Caring for Smiles in Sukkur Since 2023

Daniyal Dental Clinic was founded with a single vision: to provide world-class dental care in a warm, welcoming environment that patients feel comfortable in. Over the years, we have grown into one of Sukkur's most trusted dental practices.

We combine modern technology with a genuinely compassionate approach, ensuring every patient receives personalized, high-quality treatment — from their very first visit to the last.

"Our mission is to transform lives one smile at a time, using cutting-edge dental techniques delivered with empathy, honesty, and the highest standards of care."

‍

Dr. Naeem Ahmed

BDS, FCPS (Orthodontics) — Lead Dentist & Founder

Dr. Naeem Ahmed brings over 5 years of clinical experience to the practice. After completing his BDS from Dow University of Health Sciences and his Fellowship in Orthodontics, he returned to Sukkur to serve the local community. He is passionate about pain-free dentistry and continues to update his skills with international training programs.
